❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🎹 Do I need to provide a piano?

If your venue has a piano available (acoustic or digital), that's perfect! Many churches, hotels, and event venues have pianos on-site. If no piano is available, I can arrange for a professional digital piano rental for an additional fee of $75-$125 depending on the venue setup requirements.

📍 Do you charge travel fees?

No travel fees within 20 miles of Suwanee, GA. For venues beyond that radius, a modest travel fee of $1 per mile (round trip) applies. Popular areas like Atlanta, Alpharetta, Johns Creek, and Cumming are all within the standard service area.

🎵 Can you learn specific songs for my wedding?

Absolutely! I love creating custom arrangements. Please provide song requests at least 3-4 weeks before your event. There's no additional fee for up to 3 special song requests - it's part of making your day uniquely yours.

⏰ How early do you arrive for setup?

I arrive 30-45 minutes before the performance start time for setup and sound check. This ensures everything is perfect and ready to go without any stress on your special day.

💼 What's included in your service?

Your booking includes pre-event consultation, custom song arrangements (up to 3 requests), professional performance, all music and sheet music, and coordination with your wedding planner or venue coordinator. Equipment rental and extended travel are additional if needed.

📅 How far in advance should I book?

I recommend booking 3-6 months in advance, especially for popular wedding dates (spring and fall). However, I can often accommodate shorter notice bookings depending on availability.
